Kozhikode


Kozhikode, historically significant and culturally rich, offers a blend of history, beaches, and natural beauty. Kozhikode Beach, an iconic shoreline, provides a perfect blend of leisure and scenic views. Beypore, a historic port town, showcases Kerala's maritime history. Kappad Beach, where Vasco da Gama set foot, is steeped in historical significance. Thusharagiri Waterfalls and Mananchira Square offer scenic beauty and leisurely strolls. Pazhassi Raja Museum narrates the region's history, while Kakkayam Dam and Kozhippara Waterfalls captivate with their natural splendor.
