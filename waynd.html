Wayanad


Wayanad, known for its pristine beauty and rich biodiversity, is a nature lover's paradise. Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary houses diverse flora and fauna, while Chembra Peak offers a challenging trek. Banasura Sagar Dam, a majestic reservoir, captivates with its scenic surroundings. Edakkal Caves, an archaeological wonder, depict ancient rock art. Thirunelli Temple and Pookode Lake hold religious and scenic importance, respectively. Soochipara Waterfalls, Lakkidi View Point, and Meenmutty Waterfalls add to the district's natural allure.
