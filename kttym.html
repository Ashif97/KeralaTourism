Kottayam


Kottayam, nestled amidst serene landscapes and cultural heritage, offers a blend of spirituality and natural beauty. The district boasts the Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary, a haven for bird enthusiasts. Vaikom Mahadeva Temple and Ettumanoor Mahadeva Temple showcase architectural brilliance and spiritual significance. Illikkal Kallu, a scenic trekking spot, offers panoramic views, while Vagamon, a serene hill station, beckons with its lush greenery. Vembanad Lake, the largest in Kerala, adds to the district's charm, along with the scenic Poonjar Palace and the cascading Aruvikkuzhi Waterfalls.
