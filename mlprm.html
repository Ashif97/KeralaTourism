Malappuram


Malappuram, known for its cultural richness and natural beauty, offers a glimpse into Kerala's traditions. Kadalundi Bird Sanctuary, a haven for birdwatchers, showcases diverse avian species. Nilambur Teak Museum and Kottakkunnu add to the district's historical and cultural significance. Biyyam Kayal Boat Club offers serene boating experiences, while Teak Town in Nilambur depicts Kerala's timber history. Thirunavaya Nava Mukunda Temple and Adyanpara Waterfalls are cultural and natural marvels, respectively.
